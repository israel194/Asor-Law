About the Firm

Israel Asor, Adv. (C.P.A.)

Asor & Co. Law Firm is located in Ram Tower, Jerusalem, and includes eight attorneys with deep expertise in real estate transactions, urban renewal, and real estate taxation.

The firm specializes in representing residents in Pinui Binui (evacuation-construction) and TAMA 38 demolition-reconstruction projects — from early planning stages through final agreements and obtaining building permits.

  • Chairman of the Urban Renewal Committee — Jerusalem Bar Association
  • Deputy Chair, Real Estate Taxation Committee — Jerusalem Bar Association
  • Member, Liaison Committee with the Registrar of Corporations — Institute of CPAs
